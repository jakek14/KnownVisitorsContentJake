## Post

Meta updated its AI delivery algorithm in early March.

No announcement. No warning. Campaigns just started performing differently.

CPMs climbed 15–40% across retail, e-commerce, and lead generation. The optimization model changed overnight — and campaigns built around clicks got deprioritized in favor of predicted downstream conversions.

The natural response: test new creative. Adjust bids. Review audience targeting.

What rarely gets asked: of the people who clicked through — who visited your site, read your pricing page, compared you to a competitor — who were they?

You paid more to get them there. And you still can't follow up.

Rising ad costs don't just squeeze margins. They make every unidentified visitor more expensive to waste.

When platforms raise their floor, the brands that come out ahead aren't always the ones with better creative.

They're the ones who know who showed up — and can do something about it.

That's the problem KnownVisitors was built to solve.
