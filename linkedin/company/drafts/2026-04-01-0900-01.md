## Post

Google just made AI Max for Search available to every advertiser.

Their claim: 14–27% more conversions at similar cost.

Independent data from 250+ campaigns: 84% of advertisers saw neutral or negative results. Median CPA up 16%.

---

That gap — between what the platform promises and what actually happens in the account — is the defining challenge in digital advertising right now.

AI is genuinely improving some outcomes. For the right accounts, with the right setup, AI Max delivers real lifts. But for the majority of advertisers who tested it, the reality was more complicated.

The playbook: run AI Max as a controlled test before scaling budget. Give it 4–6 weeks, a dedicated campaign, solid conversion tracking. Let the data tell you — not the platform.

---

Here's the harder truth no one talks about: even when paid performance improves, most brands are still leaving pipeline on the floor.

You optimize the click. You reduce CPA. The visitor lands on your site, browses, and leaves.

And you still don't know who they were.

That's the layer most ad optimization strategies never touch. It's also where some of the highest-ROI pipeline lives — the visitors who showed intent but never raised their hand.
