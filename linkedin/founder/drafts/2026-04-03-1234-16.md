## Post

One marketer is doing the work of five right now.

Not because they're superhuman.

Because the tools caught up.

AI handles the copy. The creative. The A/B testing. The reporting. The analytics interpretation.

What's left is judgment — and the right data to exercise it well.

Here's what I keep coming back to when thinking about what this shift actually means:

The 1-person marketing team doesn't need more data.

They need better data.

Give them a firehose and they drown. Give them signal and they move.

Think about the difference: a spreadsheet of 50,000 companies that "visited your site" vs. "this person was on your pricing page twice this week, their email is verified, they're not on the DNC list."

One is homework. The other is a conversation starter.

This is actually the core thesis I built KnownVisitors around.

The teams winning right now aren't the ones with the biggest data warehouse.

They're the ones who know exactly who showed up, confirmed that person is reachable, and acted on it the same day.

The 10X Marketer era rewards precision over volume.

The tools that win in this environment aren't the most feature-rich ones — they're the ones that hand you clean signal with no cleanup work required.

What's the most underrated tool in your lean marketing stack right now?
