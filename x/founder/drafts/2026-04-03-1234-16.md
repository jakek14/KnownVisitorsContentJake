## Post

VC money is forgiving.
Your own money isn't.

That's why bootstrapped founders make faster decisions.

Every wrong call, you feel immediately.

No softening. No buffer. No runway extension clause.

Skin in the game doesn't just change what you do.

It changes how you think.
