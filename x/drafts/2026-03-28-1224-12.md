## Context

Forrester data (surfaced in multiple 2026 B2B lead gen reports) shows the B2B buying process is almost entirely self-directed before any sales contact happens. Combined: buyers show up to your site with intent, do their due diligence, and leave — often without ever filling out a form. The brand that can identify those visitors and follow up has a massive edge. This is a short, punchy problem-awareness post that naturally ties into KnownVisitors without a hard pitch. The last line lands as a founder authentically referencing the pain that led them to build something — not a CTA. Five previous drafts had no KV mentions, so this is appropriate for the ~20% cadence.
