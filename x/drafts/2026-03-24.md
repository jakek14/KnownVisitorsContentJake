## Post

Google added AI voice-overs to your Performance Max ads on March 20.

If you didn't manually opt out of every single campaign before the deadline, Google is now generating audio from your ad copy and layering it over your videos.

No email blast. No warning. Just: here, we improved your ads for you.

This is not a mistake. This is the product.

Google is slowly becoming the creative director of your entire ad account.

## Context

Google's opt-out-only AI voice-over rollout for Performance Max landed March 20. With PMax now handling 62% of all Google ad clicks, this touches most advertisers' budgets. The broader trend: Google keeps automating (and taking ownership of) ad creative — targeting, bidding, copy, now audio. Strong hot take for any founder or marketer running paid ads. The "opt-out by default" model is increasingly how Google operates, and most people miss these changes entirely.
