## Post

D2C brands now lose $29 on every new customer they acquire.

28% of first-time buyers ever come back.

The whole model depends on a customer who statistically won't return.

And the industry's response? Spend more on acquisition.

More Meta. More Google. Higher CPMs. Bigger audiences.

Nobody's asking: what about everyone who already showed up but didn't buy?

The leaky bucket problem isn't a traffic problem. It's a visibility problem.
