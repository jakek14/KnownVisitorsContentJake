## Post

79% of your store's traffic is on mobile.

Mobile converts at 1.8%.
Desktop converts at 3.9%.

That gap is your revenue leak.

Not your ad creative. Not your targeting. Not your bidding strategy.

You're spending money to drive traffic to a checkout most people won't finish.

Fix the funnel you already have before you buy another click.

## Context

Shopify's 2026 data confirms a massive and largely ignored split: mobile dominates traffic share but converts at less than half the rate of desktop. Most brands are laser-focused on ad optimization while ignoring the fundamental UX problem sitting right in front of them. This is a reframe post — redirecting attention from acquisition spend to conversion fundamentals. Timely because e-commerce brands are under serious CAC pressure (up 222% over 8 years, losing $29/customer on average) and most are still looking for the answer in ads rather than on-site experience.
