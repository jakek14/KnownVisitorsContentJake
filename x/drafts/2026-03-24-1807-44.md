## Post

OpenAI is charging $60 CPM for ads inside ChatGPT.

That's 3x the standard Meta rate.

And you need a $200,000 minimum just to get in the beta.

Every marketer is scrambling for the next channel while they still can't tell you who visited their site yesterday.

New inventory doesn't fix a broken funnel.

## Context

On March 2, 2026, Criteo formally announced it joined OpenAI's advertising pilot as the first major ad-tech platform to integrate with ChatGPT inventory. OpenAI is charging ~$60 CPM with a $200k minimum commitment. This is a provocative, timely moment to challenge the instinct of always chasing the next shiny channel instead of working the traffic you already have — which is exactly the world KnownVisitors operates in.
