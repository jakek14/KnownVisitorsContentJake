## Post

CAC is up 40–60% since 2023.

The average D2C brand loses $29 on every new customer they acquire.

Only 28% of first-time buyers ever come back.

Everyone's response: better creative, smarter bidding, more A/B tests.

But the traffic already came. You paid for it. It bounced. You have no idea who it was.

You can't retarget a mystery.

Fix the data before you try to fix the ads.
